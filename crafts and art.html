Basotho Crafts & Art

From intricately woven baskets to symbolic beadwork and traditional pottery, Basotho crafts reflect creativity, identity, and heritage passed down through generations.

Woven Baskets

Made from grass and reeds, Basotho baskets are functional and decorative, often used in homes and markets.

Pottery

Clay pots serve both practical purposes and ceremonial roles, with designs that tell cultural stories.

Beadwork

Beads are used to create jewelry and adornments, often carrying symbolic meanings of status, age, or tradition.

Textile & Tapestry
